Rule #1Objective

The objective of the game is to win all the cards or most of them.

Rule #2Number of Players

The game can be played with two players.

Rule #3Setup

The deck is shuffled and divided evenly between the players. Each player takes their half of the deck and holds it face down, creating their own draw pile.

Rule #4Gameplay

Both players simultaneously reveal the top card from their draw pile and place it face up on the table.

The player who reveals the card with the higher rank (see below for ranking) wins both cards and adds them to the bottom of their own draw pile.

If there is a tie (i.e., both players reveal cards of the same rank), it's called a "war".

In a war, each player places three additional cards face down on the table, followed by one card face up. The player with the higher face-up card wins all the cards on the table and adds them to the bottom of their draw pile.

If there's another tie during a war, the process repeats until one player's face-up card is higher than the other's.

Rule #5Card Ranking

Cards are ranked in descending order from Ace (high) to 2 (low) based on their face value.

The suit of the cards (hearts, diamonds, clubs, spades) does not matter in this game.

Rule #6Winning

Some variations of the game may include different rules for handling ties during a war or special rules for certain card combinations.
